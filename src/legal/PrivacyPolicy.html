I. Introduction

At MealOps, we connect individuals with the best meal programs and services available, enabling users to meet their dietary and nutritional needs with ease and convenience. In turn, we help third-party companies and athletic programs efficiently manage and offer their meal-related services.

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how MealOps, Inc. and its affiliates (“MealOps”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collect, process, retain, and disclose your Personal Information when providing its services, including, but not limited to, its consumer platforms, websites, and mobile applications, any website, platform, or application that contains or includes a link to this Policy, and channels of communication (collectively the “Services”).

This Policy does not apply to personnel of athletic programs or third-party companies using our services, which may be subject to separate privacy policies.

II. What Personal Information does MealOps collect or process?

Personal Information is any information that MealOps can reasonably use to identify you, whether that information identifies you on its own or when combined with other information. Anonymous information and information that has been de-identified such that it cannot identify you is not Personal Information.

We may process the following categories of Personal Information in connection with our Services:

  • Contact Information, including name, address (such as home address or other preferred contact address), email address(es), phone number(s), and other contact information provided to us.
  • Account and Profile Information, including username and credentials, privacy settings and notification preferences, pictures, and other information you add to your MealOps profile, any subscription information, and identifiers that we may link to an account and information received from third-party platforms or authentication services that are linked to our Services.
  • Identification Documentation for Verification (Limited Circumstances), including government-issued identification documents, which we may process in limited circumstances for legal and regulatory compliance, such as to verify age for specific meal programs or services, or for fraud prevention.
  • Communications with MealOps and Program/Company Representatives, including the content of in-app messages, communications through or in connection with our Services (including, without limitation, through our and third-party platforms), SMS/text messages, chats, phone calls, and emails with us related to our Services, customer support inquiries and outreach, the date and time of communications, method or channel used, call recordings (if applicable), the outcomes, actions, and/or resolutions from the communications, and other information.
  • Content you create or share, including reviews about experiences on our platform, such as ratings of a meal program or third-party company you interact with, or photos you upload.
  • Payment Information, including credit card or debit card information and information about the payment methods and services (such as PayPal or Venmo) used to make purchases or subscribe to services on or through our Services. Payment information is primarily stored and processed by our third-party service providers, including our payment processors; however, we may store and process limited payment-related information such as the last four digits of a payment card, name of the financial institution, and payment service.
  • Activity and Transactions on or with our Services, including information about the meal programs or services subscribed to, service history, items placed in the cart, special instructions included in a subscription or order (e.g., dietary preferences, delivery preferences), transaction amounts, promotional codes and discounts used and received, the programs or companies subscribed from, gift cards ordered, the features and functionality used or interacted with, searches and actions on our Services (including through using session replay technology), responses to our surveys or other market research activities, participation in sweepstakes, promotions, or contests, photos and/or videos that you may send or provide to us related to your activity and transactions on or with our Services, and use of gifts, our referral program or other special programs (including information about those you have sent or received gifts to or from or referred).
  • Geolocation Information, if you have consented by enabling location access, we may receive and store your precise location information, including when our apps are running in the foreground (our app is open and on screen) or background (our app is open, not on screen) of your device. You may use our Services without enabling the collection of precise location information from your device; however, this may impact your experience including the accuracy of service delivery and/or the availability of certain content, features, and functionality (e.g., you may have to manually enter your address). If you have not allowed us to receive your precise location, we can only determine your approximate location. To opt-out of location sharing after turning it on, please refer to your device's settings or the privacy settings within the MealOps app, if available.
  • Device Information, including information about the computers, phones, and other devices you use to interact with MealOps or our Services, internet-protocol address, device-based identifiers, operating system and version, preferred language, hardware model identifiers, browser information including type and settings, and other device information.
  • Information from Co-Branded or Third-Party Offers, we may collect information that you use to register for, and your activity with, third parties, in connection with co-branded or third-party offers from us and our partners, such as when you enroll for co-branded credit card opportunities with our credit card partners.

Some of the information identified above that you choose to provide us (specifically, government-issued identification, which we receive when we verify age and identity for certain services to prevent fraud and demonstrate legal and regulatory compliance, and precise location information, which we receive to facilitate service delivery and certain content, features, and functionality within our Services), may be considered sensitive Personal Information under the laws of some jurisdictions. You can turn off MealOps’s ability to collect and use precise location at any time in your app settings. We do not use any sensitive Personal Information for purposes other than as reasonably expected by an average consumer to provide our Services, for legal, security and safety purposes, and without the purpose of inferring characteristics about an individual. Where legally required, MealOps will collect and process sensitive Personal Information only with consent.

When we process de-identified information, we maintain and use the information in de-identified form, and do not attempt to re-identify the information, except as permitted by applicable law.
